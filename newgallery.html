What is an Intermediate-Mass Black Hole?

Scientists haven’t been able to find any intermediate-mass black holes (IMBHs). These black holes are between the well-studied star black holes, which have masses of up to a few tens of solar masses, and the huge supermassive black holes that are found at the centers of galaxies.
